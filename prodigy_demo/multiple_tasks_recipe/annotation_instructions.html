ANNOTATION INSTRUCTIONS

How to annotate:

Instructions for annotators....

Save your work:

- Always click on the SAVE button after you finish annotating for the day;

- You can also click SAVE after annotating a couple of articles to make sure you don't loose your work;

Accept, Reject and Ignore buttons:

- Accept(A): accept annotation if forum post is weather related;

- Reject(X): reject annotation if forum post is not weather related;

- Ignore(space bar): ignore annotation if you're unsure if it's weather related.

Redo annotation:

- Select the sentence for which you want to redo the annotation by clicking on it on the panel on the left hand side below HISTORY.
